The Hotel

The luxurious San Clemente Palace Kempinski in Venice will play host to you during your stay, and sits on a private island just minutes away from Piazza San Marco.

Easily accessible by a complimentary boat service, the heart of Venice is right on your doorstep, where you can wander through stunning architecture, take a Gondola ride on the Grand Canal, and explore boutique shopping in the city.

As you arrive in Venice, you’ll make your way to a proud member of the Leading Hotels of the World, where you can enjoy this exclusive retreat whilst taking in the authentic Venetian style and surroundings.

The hotel itself boasts 190 classically decorated rooms and suites, overlooking either the lagoon or centuries-old park. When it comes to cuisine, choose from the hotel’s three indulgent restaurants – Acquerello, La Dolce, and Insieme – for a culinary experience that will delight your senses.
