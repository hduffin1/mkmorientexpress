The Train

Many have heard of the Orient Express, but few are lucky enough to experience it. From the moment you step on board the train, you’ll be transported back in time as you are welcomed with the glamour and elegance of the ‘Golden Age’ of European travel.

During your trip, you’ll enjoy outstanding views of the Kentish countryside and Italian Dolomites, all whilst experiencing an exquisite first class service.

From delicious meals created by skilled chefs and a relaxing Bellini brunch, to your own private cabin and a social bar car – this journey has been designed for you to sit back, relax, and take in the opulence and grandeur of the Orient Express.
