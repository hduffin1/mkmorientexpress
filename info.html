Additional Information

Dress Code

On board the Orient Express
Smart daywear is appropriate on board the train, please do not wear jeans, T-shirts or trainers.


Orient Express evening meal
A minimum of a jacket and tie for gentlemen and the equivalent for ladies. Such is the ambience of the train you may wish to wear your most glamorous finery.


Time at leisure
Casual clothing


Farewell Party
For the final evening of glitz and glamour gentlemen are requested to wear black-tie and ladies cocktail dresses or evening gowns.

Luggage

For your journey 3 x pieces of luggage will be required, please read the below information carefully and contact the MKM Event Team should you have any questions.


Hand Luggage
1 piece of hand luggage will be allowed per person, for example a handbag. This should contain your passport and travel documents and will be kept on your person throughout the journey.


Orient Express Cabin Luggage
A maximum allowance of 10kg per person. This should be either a cabin sized bag, overnight bag or suit carrier. This item will be checked in at Doncaster airport for the flight and will be waiting for you in your cabin when you board the train. Please make sure your attire for the evening on board the train is packed within this luggage item.


Our suggestion for this element of the trip would be to travel with 1 suit carrier and 1 overnight/cabin bag per couple along with your hand luggage for personal items.


Venice Hotel Luggage
A maximum allowance of 10kg per person. This luggage will be checked in at Doncaster airport and transferred straight to the Hotel in Venice ahead of your arrival.


Our suggestion would be to check in one large 20kg case per couple to be transferred directly to the hotel in Venice. For the return flight the maximum weight of all combined luggage per person is 20kg. Based on this there will be no facility to pay for any excess baggage upon check in due to weight limits of the charter aircraft.
