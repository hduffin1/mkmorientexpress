Luggage

For your journey 3 x pieces of luggage will be required, please read the below information carefully and contact the MKM Event Team should you have any questions.


Hand Luggage
1 piece of hand luggage will be allowed per person, for example a handbag. This should contain your passport and travel documents and will be kept on your person throughout the journey.


Orient Express Cabin Luggage
A maximum allowance of 10kg per person. This should be either a cabin sized bag, overnight bag or suit carrier. This item will be checked in at Doncaster airport for the flight and remain with you for your overnight on-board the train. Please make sure your attire for the evening meal is packed within this luggage item.


Our suggestion for this element of the trip would be to travel with 1 suit carrier for evening attire and 1 overnight/cabin bag per couple along with your hand luggage for personal items.


Venice Hotel Luggage
A maximum allowance of 10kg per person. This luggage will be checked in at Doncaster airport and transferred straight to the Hotel in Venice ahead of your arrival.


Please note the charter flights have strict luggage allowances, the maximum weight of all combined luggage per person is 20kg. Based on this there will be no facility to pay for any excess baggage upon check in due to weight limits of the aircraft.
