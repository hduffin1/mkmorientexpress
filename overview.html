Overview

In order to celebrate MKM’s milestone 25th anniversary, we are inviting you to join us on a once-in-a-lifetime journey aboard the world-famous Orient Express from Calais to Venice.

After a chartered flight to Lille, you’ll enjoy a scenic route to board the Orient Express in Calais. Following a luxurious time aboard the world-famous Orient Express, you’ll arrive at your destination where you’ll be staying at the Belmond Hotel Cipriani – a heavenly hideaway in the beating heart of Venice.

Venice promises a magnificent adventure in the ‘City of Canals’ – arguably one of the most beautiful and alluring cities in the world. During your stay, we’ll make the most of its vibrant and affluent spirit, with time at leisure to explore and a lavish farewell party.

Thank you for your loyal and continued custom, and we can’t wait for you to join us on this incredible trip.
