Overview

In order to celebrate MKM’s milestone 25th anniversary, we are inviting you to join us on a once-in-a-lifetime journey aboard the world-famous Orient Express from London to Venice.

Staying aboard the British Pullman for a night before arriving at the San Clemente Palace in Venice, you’ll experience nothing but comfort and enchantment as your private on board cabin is transformed into a cosy bedroom – allowing you to relax at every point of the journey.

The end destination of Venice is one that promises a magnificent adventure in the ‘City of Canals’ – arguably one of the most beautiful and alluring cities in the world.

Whilst in Venice, we’ll make the most of its vibrant and affluent spirit, enjoying a scenic drinks reception and lavish gala dinner during the two-night stay.

Thank you for your loyal and continued custom, and we can’t wait for you to join us on this incredible trip.
